SuperGPT does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.

1. Our Privacy Commitment

At SuperGPT, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our browser extension.

Key Principle: SuperGPT operates entirely locally on your device and does not collect any personal data.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

SuperGPT does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Your ChatGPT conversations or prompts
  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Browser history or browsing data
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

3. How SuperGPT Works

SuperGPT operates entirely on your local device:

  • All processing happens in your browser
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • Settings and preferences are stored locally
  • The extension only modifies the ChatGPT interface visually
Local Operation: SuperGPT works by adding CSS styles and JavaScript functionality to the ChatGPT webpage. No data leaves your device.

4. Data Storage

Any data stored by SuperGPT is kept locally on your device:

Data Type Storage Location Purpose
Extension Settings Browser Local Storage Remember your preferences
Color Preferences Browser Local Storage Customize appearance
Typography Settings Browser Local Storage Font size preferences

All stored data is local to your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the extension or clearing your browser data.

5. Third-Party Services

SuperGPT does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data. However, please note:

  • SuperGPT enhances the ChatGPT website, which has its own privacy policy
  • We are not affiliated with OpenAI or ChatGPT
  • We do not have access to your ChatGPT account or conversations
  • Any data collection by ChatGPT is governed by OpenAI's privacy policy

6. Browser Permissions

SuperGPT requires minimal browser permissions:

  • Active Tab Access: To modify the ChatGPT interface
  • Storage Permission: To save your local preferences

These permissions are necessary for the extension to function and do not involve data collection or transmission.

7. Children's Privacy

SuperGPT does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect no data, we comply with all children's privacy regulations.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Since we don't collect data, changes to this policy will not affect your privacy rights.

9. Your Rights

Since SuperGPT doesn't collect personal data, you have complete control:

  • No data to access, correct, or delete
  • No data portability concerns
  • Complete privacy by default
  • Uninstall the extension to remove all local data
